Hongsun Jang

I am a graduate student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Seoul National University. My research interests are mainly focusing on distributed learning and building efficient neural network architecture by neural architecture search (NAS).

I was a engineering intern at Samsung Electronics. I received my B.S. in Computer Science from Yonsei University.
